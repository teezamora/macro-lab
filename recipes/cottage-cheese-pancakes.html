⚡ Men's Physique Notes
Low-calorie breakfast for contest prep: At 100 kcal and 5.6g protein per pancake, these fit cleanly into a caloric deficit. Stack 3 for a 300 kcal, 17g protein breakfast — leaving room for other meals without blowing your daily target.

Great for maintaining conditioning: The low fat content (4.6g per pancake) is ideal when you're trying to stay lean between shows. These won't spike dietary fat the way eggs and bacon would, keeping you on track for visible abs year-round.

Cut the fat further on prep: Butter is the biggest fat contributor (46g for the full batch). Replace with 2 tbsp of non-fat Greek yogurt to drop roughly 25g fat across the batch — useful in the final 8–10 weeks before a show.

Carb timing for upper body days: With ~9g carbs per pancake, these are a solid pre-workout option on upper body days (chest, shoulders, back) when you want a moderate carb spike without going overboard. Eat 3–4 about 60–90 minutes before training.

Sodium watch: Baking powder adds ~1,464mg of sodium to the batch. On peak week or water-cut phases, halving the baking powder reduces sodium without noticeably affecting texture.
