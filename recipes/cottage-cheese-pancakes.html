⚡ Bodybuilder Notes
High-protein win: Cottage cheese + eggs deliver ~88g protein for the whole batch (~11g/pancake) — a solid post-workout carb+protein combo.

Fat is elevated mostly from butter (46g). Swap butter for Greek yogurt or reduce to 2 tbsp to cut ~200 kcal and push the macro ratio more protein-forward.

Sodium alert: Baking powder accounts for 1,567mg of the 3,541mg total sodium. If you're cutting or watching sodium, halve the baking powder.

Timing tip: With ~19g carbs/pancake, this works best as a pre- or post-workout meal. Pair 2–3 pancakes with a protein shake for a complete anabolic window hit.
